Process To Join Sangharsh Dhol Tasha Pathak



1. Click Here And Whatsapp Us Your Details Like Name, Profession and Area You Live In.
2. You'll get a Reply with all the further Instructions.
3. You'll Need to Attend a Pathak Meeting for Further Registration Process.
4. After Completing the above steps you can download the Registration Form From Below Button, Take a Back to Back print of the Form, Fill and Submit the Form.
